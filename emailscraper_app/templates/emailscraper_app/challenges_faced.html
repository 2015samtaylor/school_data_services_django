Problems Districts Face with Data & How We Help

Data Silos

Challenges Faced: Data is often scattered across different platforms, making integration with the Student Information System difficult and hindering year-over-year analytics.

How We Help: We streamline data collection and integration through automated data pipelines. We ensure that schools have a unified, real time, accurate, accessible data ecosystem.

Data Quality and Accuracy

Challenges Faced: Manual data entry can be draining, and potentially can lead to mistakes. Within these processes data validation efforts can be lost in translation. This can impact compliance, and actionable insights.

How We Help: Our processes automate data entry and validation, ensuring accuracy and compliance with time to focus on actionable insights.

Data Security and Privacy

Challenges Faced: When handling sensitive data, data security and privacy should be at the forefront. Outdated security measures can be a risk.

How We Help: We leverage Google Cloud Platform's advanced security infrastructure to safeguard sensitive student and district data, ensuring compliance and protection against breaches.

Data Access and Sharing

Challenges Faced: In any organization it can be hard to know where to go for data, making data access and sharing a formidable challenge.

How We Help: Our processes enable real-time data access and dashboards, ensuring that authorized users can instantly retrieve and interact with the information they need for informed decision-making. Say goodbye to static reports."

Data Overload

Challenges Faced: Year over year, districts accumulate massive amounts of data, making it increasingly difficult to extract meaningful insights and track progress over time.

How We Help: We provide scalable, organized data warehouses that efficiently manage and structure large datasets, enabling districts to make sense of their data and gain actionable insights that drive student success.
