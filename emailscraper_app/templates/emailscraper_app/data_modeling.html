Data Modeling and Centralized Metrics (More to Come)

Data modeling is the foundation of effective data management. By normalizing data, we ensure that redundant and inconsistent data is eliminated, making it easier to manage and analyze. Normalization involves organizing data into structured tables, reducing duplication, and ensuring relationships between data points are clearly defined.

An example is shown below where attendance data and grade is normalized into separate tables, and then brought together into a centralized table that analysts can use to gain insights. This approach ensures that your data is clean, reliable, and ready for analysis.
